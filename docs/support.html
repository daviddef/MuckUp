Grub Support

Spot it, log it, clean it up — help with the app.

Common questions

How do I report a muck (litter or hazard)?

Tap "Raise a Muck" on the Home tab, take or choose a photo, add a short description, and drop the pin at the location. It appears on the map for others nearby immediately.

Why can't I see mucks near me?

Grub needs location access to show nearby reports. Check iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Grub, and make sure it's set to "While Using the App." You can also adjust your search radius by double-tapping the location icon on the Find tab.

How does my Grub companion grow?

Your Grub progresses through life stages as your real-world impact — mucks raised, mucks cleared, events attended — adds up. Keep a streak going to see it grow fastest.

What's a Squad?

A Squad is a group you form with friends, classmates, or your local community. Squad members share a leaderboard and can coordinate on events together.

My data isn't syncing across devices

Syncing requires Sign in with Apple and an active iCloud account on the device. If you're using Guest mode, your data intentionally stays local to that device only. Check Settings → [your name] → iCloud is signed in, then relaunch Grub.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap the flag icon on any muck, event, or profile to flag it for review. Flagged content is hidden pending moderation.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account data. See our Privacy Policy for details on what's stored.
