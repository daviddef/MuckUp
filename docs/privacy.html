Privacy Policy — Grub

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Grub ("we", "the app") helps you report litter and hazards, join community clean-up events, and track your local impact. This page explains what data Grub collects, why, and how it's handled.

What Grub collects

  • Location. Used to show mucks, hazards, and events near you, and to power the "near me" search. Location is only used while you're using the app.
  • Photos. If you attach a photo to a muck you raise, that photo is uploaded and becomes visible to other users viewing that muck.
  • Account info. If you sign in with Apple, we receive the identifier Apple provides (and your name/email only if you choose to share them) so your mucks, Squad membership, and streaks sync across your devices via iCloud.
  • Content you post. Muck reports, descriptions, event details, and Squad activity you create are stored so other users can see and act on them.

What Grub does not do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use your data for third-party advertising.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

Guest mode

If you continue as a guest instead of signing in, your data stays on your device only and is not synced or shared with our backend beyond what's needed to show public mucks/events on the map.

Where your data lives

Account-linked data (mucks, Squads, streaks, badges) syncs via Apple's iCloud/CloudKit under your own iCloud account. Public muck and event data (what's needed to show reports on the shared map) is stored so other nearby users can see it.

Moderation

Grub includes a flag/hide feature so users can report inappropriate content. Flagged content is hidden pending review.

Your choices

  • You can deny location access at any time in iOS Settings — the map and "near me" features will be limited accordingly.
  • You can delete your account and associated data from within the app's Settings.
  • You can use Grub without an account via Guest mode.

Children

Grub is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
